Mission

Help businesses access suitable capital with confidence.

Our mission is to turn scattered funding choices into clear product recommendations, practical preparation steps, and transparent lender conversations.

Vision

A funding market where fit matters more than friction.

We believe responsible financing should be simple to compare, easy to understand, and aligned with how each business actually earns revenue.

Our story

Built after seeing good companies choose the wrong capital.

Many operators know they need funding, but not which product, amount, tenure, or repayment structure fits their reality. NIKIT was created to bring underwriting logic, product education, and partner access into one clean experience.

Why we started

Founders were losing time to repeated forms. CFOs were comparing offers without standardized terms. Lenders were receiving applications that did not match their criteria. NIKIT closes that gap before the formal process begins.
